About us

Travel is better when you already
know somebody there.

You were going to travel anyway. Meet Me There just changes who you stay with. We're a referral-only circle of professionals, creatives, and curious people — the kind of people who have considerably good taste, interesting travel stories, and a life worth visiting, even if only for a long weekend. This isn't just finding a cheap place to sleep. It's about expanding your network into a different city before you even board the plane. Friends of friends, opening their doors. That's the whole idea.

No more breaking the bank

No payment to stay

This is a home swap network, not a booking platform. Staying with another member costs nothing. The one fee: a professional cleaning charge before your arrival — we coordinate it directly so the space is ready when you land.

A note on sharing

We know you value your space.

Letting someone into your home while you're away is not a small thing — and we're not going to pretend it is. You've curated your space. You have a system. You like things the way they are.

But what makes Meet Me There different is that the people in this circle feel exactly the same way about their own homes. They're the ones who light a candle when they get home, who spend every Sunday cleaning, or who spent three months deciding on the perfect sofa. They're not reckless guests — they're people who understand what it means to take care of something beautiful because they do it themselves.

What you get in return is the chance to see a new city through the eyes of someone who actually lives there. Not a hotel room. Not a short-term rental. A real home, with real taste, owned by someone who gets it. That's the trade. And for the right person, it's more than worth it.
